# Colors

Colors are a crucial aspect of brand identity, they can evoke emotions and create a visual hierarchy.<br />
When selecting colors, it's important to align them with its messaging and values.<br />
It's also important to ensure the colors chosen can be easily reproduced across various mediums and platforms.
